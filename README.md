Ecommerce Superstore Sales Analysis
Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the Superstore Sales dataset using Power BI. The goal was to identify key business insights related to sales, profit, customer segments, and operational efficiency. The dashboard consolidates multiple KPIs and visualizations into a single interactive view, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Problem Analysis
Businesses often lack clarity on which product categories and sub-categories contribute most to revenue and profitability.

Limited visibility into customer segments, payment preferences, and shipping modes hinders strategic planning.

Year-over-year performance comparisons are essential to track growth trends but are not easily accessible in raw datasets.

Problem Solved
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of sales and profit metrics.

Enabled trend analysis through monthly and yearly comparisons of sales and profit.

Highlighted top-performing categories (Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture) and sub-categories (Chairs, Phones, Binders).

Delivered insights into customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) and shipping preferences (Standard, Second Class, First Class, Same Day).

Incorporated forecasting visuals to project future sales trends.

Functions and Features Used
DAX measures for calculating KPIs such as Sum of Sales, Sum of Profit, Average Delivery Time, and Quantity.

Donut and bar charts for category, payment mode, and segment analysis.

Line charts for year-wise monthly sales and profit comparison.

Forecasting visuals to predict future performance.

Interactive filters for segment-specific analysis.
